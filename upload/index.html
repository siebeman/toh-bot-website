Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

TOH Bot is a Discord bot. When you use slash commands, Discord sends us the command input you provide (such as level numbers or dates). We do not store this data — commands are processed and the response is returned immediately.

  • We do not store your Discord user ID, username, or profile information
  • We do not log command history or track usage patterns
  • We do not share any data with third parties

Race Mode

If you use the Race Mode feature and connect to a Supabase database, the following data is stored in your own Supabase project:

  • Your Discord display name and user ID (used to identify you on the leaderboard)
  • Your Tower of Hell level data that you manually log
  • Your profile avatar URL from Discord

This data is stored in a database you own and control. We have no access to it. You can delete it at any time through your Supabase dashboard.

Accuracy disclaimer

Tower of Hell is created by YXceptional Studios. This bot is unofficial and is not affiliated with or endorsed by YXceptional Studios. Formula data is sourced from the Tower of Hell wiki and we strive for accuracy, but we do not guarantee it.
